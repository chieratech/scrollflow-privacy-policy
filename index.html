Privacy Policy for ScrollFlow

Effective Date: May 7, 2026

ScrollFlow respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user data and permissions.

Information Collection

ScrollFlow does not collect, store, or share personal information.

Accessibility Service Usage

ScrollFlow uses Android Accessibility Services to perform automatic scrolling and swipe gestures on behalf of the user.

The Accessibility Service is used strictly for:

  • Automatic scrolling
  • Swipe gesture automation
  • Hands-free device interaction

ScrollFlow does not use Accessibility Services to collect personal data, monitor activity, or read sensitive information.

Overlay Permission

ScrollFlow may request permission to display controls over other apps to allow users to pause or stop scrolling while using their device.

Third-Party Services

ScrollFlow does not use third-party analytics or advertising services.

Data Sharing

ScrollFlow does not sell, share, or transmit user data to external servers.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
